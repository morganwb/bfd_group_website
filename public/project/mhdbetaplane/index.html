Magnetohydrodynamics on the Equatorial Beta Plane

Motivation

Motivated by the discovery of active nests of convection in rapidly rotating stellar simulations, we are interested in constructing a local model that can reproduce the phenomena. In order to do this, we’re investigating the use of the equatorial beta plane.
